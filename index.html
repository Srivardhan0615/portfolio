01⭐ FeaturedPythonPandasTrading APIs

Algorithmic Market Strategy System (AMSS v5.0)

↗

A multi-strategy algorithmic trading engine for BTC/USD Perpetual contracts. Integrates trend-following, breakout detection, and market regime classification with a full risk management stack.

PythonPandasEMA IndicatorsRisk EnginePerpetual ContractsDelta Exchange
→ Problem Statement:

Manual trading and static strategies fail in volatile crypto markets. A dynamic, data-driven system was needed to handle regime shifts automatically.

→ Architecture Overview:

Multi-module pipeline: Market Regime Detection → Signal Generation → Execution Logic → Risk Management Stack. Fully automated end-to-end.

→ Market Regime Detection:

Uses EMA crossovers and volatility indicators to classify market conditions in real-time and switch between active strategy modules accordingly.

→ Risk Management Stack:

Net-delta control, dynamic position sizing, drawdown limits, and emergency exit logic ensure capital protection during adverse conditions.

→ Performance:

30-day paper trading simulation completed. System adapts strategy in real-time based on live regime classification.
